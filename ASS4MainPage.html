    At Batyr's

    Welcome to the restaurant "At Batyr's"! Our doors are open to lovers of authentic Mediterranean cuisine and an atmosphere that will take you right to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.

    Here you will find a rich variety of dishes inspired by traditional recipes from Greece, Italy, Spain and other countries in the region.

    • Our chefs use only the freshest, highest quality ingredients to create an unforgettable taste experience.
    • Our visitors can enjoy the view of the open kitchen, where every dish is prepared with love and art.
    • We also offer an impressive selection of wines from the region to complement your lunch or dinner.

    At our restaurant, every guest is valued and we pride ourselves on providing outstanding service. This is a unique place where gastronomic art combines with the magic of the atmosphere. Our restaurant provides you with a unique opportunity to embark on an unforgettable culinary journey through the splendor of tastes and aromas presented in the varied dishes of our menu.
